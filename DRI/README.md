### Description

The Dronetag DRI module simplifies your drone manufacturing process to comply with the latest Direct/Broadcast Remote Identification (RID) regulations in the EU and US.
With its ready-to-install design, the DRI module eliminates the need for R&D and testing.
Simply connect it to the drone's flight controller to meet the mandatory RID requirements.

DRI transmission is standardized according to prEN 4709-002 for the EU and ASTM F3411 for the US.
Dronetag DRI is compatible with PX4 and ArduPilot FCs (e.g., Pixhawk Cube) or any other MAVLink-based system.
