### Description

`uBlox MAX-M10S` GNSS module with the `IST8308` compass

Introducing the M10136, our most compact and lightweight GPS solution yet, engineered specifically for UAV applications where size and weight are critical. This state-of-the-art module is the direct successor to our popular mini-SAM GPS, offering enhanced features in a thinner and lighter design. Ideal for advanced drone navigation, the M10136 incorporates a reliable IST8310 compass and the high-performance uBlox-M10S L1 GNSS receiver, ensuring precise and stable positioning capabilities. With a serial interface for straightforward integration, this GPS module is perfect for developers looking to push the boundaries of UAV technology.

Features:
* Compact Design: Significantly thinner and lighter than its predecessor, making it ideal for use in small UAVs.
* Advanced Navigation: Equipped with a IST8310 compass and uBlox-M10S L1 GNSS receiver for accurate and reliable positioning.
* Serial Interface: Simplifies integration with a wide range of UAV systems.
* Enhanced Performance: Offers improved sensitivity and faster positioning speeds, perfect for dynamic UAV applications.
* Robust Build: Designed to withstand the rigors of outdoor drone operations.
