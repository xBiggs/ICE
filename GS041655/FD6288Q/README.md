### Description

FD6288 is an integrated circuit featuring three independent half-bridge gate drivers, designed for high-voltage, high-speed MOSFET driving, capable of operating at voltages up to +250V. The FD6288 incorporates VCC/VBS undervoltage lockout (UVLO) protection to prevent power transistors from operating under insufficient voltage conditions. The FD6288 features built-in shoot-through prevention and dead-time control to protect against shoot-through in the driven high-side and low-side MOSFETs, effectively safeguarding power devices. The FD6288 also integrates input signal filtering to prevent interference from input noise.
